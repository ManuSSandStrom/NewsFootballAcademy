About Our Academy

The News Football Academy is an inclusive institution that welcomes individuals from all over the world to join and be a part of their football community. The academy’s logo, featuring a compass with the words ‘North East West South’ and ‘Football Unites,’ symbolizes their commitment to bringing people together through the sport.

The academy’s motto, ‘No racism, no discrimination, just one heart, one aim, and love for the game,’ reflects their values of inclusivity, equality, and passion for football. It strives to create a welcoming environment where individuals from diverse backgrounds can come together to share their love for the game.

At News Football Academy, our mission is to provide a nurturing environment where young footballers can develop their skills, build character, and achieve their full potential. We strive to create a culture of excellence, sportsmanship, and teamwork, empowering our players to succeed both on and off the field.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We pursue excellence in all aspects of football.
  • Sportsmanship: We promote respect, fair play, and good sportsmanship.
  • Teamwork: We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration.
  • Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity and ethics.
  • Passion: We ignite a passion for football in our players.

We pursue excellence in all aspects of football, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and achievement.
